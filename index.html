FAQ

Q: What is BlankOn Revival Project?
A: An attempt to bring back the long-dormant BlankOn project from the depths of /dev/null
Q: How can I contribute?
A: Until the revival is complete, we’ll be operating with technical contributors only. We’re opening positions for core contributors, but the selection process will be strict for several reasons. To become one, you’ll need to prove just how geeky you really are. We will collaborate in public Telegram group where anyone can watch and join the movements then let the contribution hapened naturally. We also desperately need donations in the form of hardware, bandwidth (server colocation), or cold hard cash. We've got transparent reports here to show where every rupiah goes. Please contact @senyumslamet if you want to donate.
Q: What can I expect from the project? What’s new in BlankOn?
A: After the revival, BlankOn will reinforce its original goal of improving people’s capabilities by opening a broader range of non-technical roles.. BlankOn will also lower contribution barriers while enforcing a stricter Code of Conduct, aimed at keeping the community healthy and contributor-friendly. Think of it as upgrading the social layer while optimizing the dev pipeline. The project will also work to maintain an optimal ratio of technical to non-technical contributors, ensuring long-term sustainability
Q: What is the success metric of the revival?
A: You can download and install a fully-fledged, brand-new BlankOn Linux image—built, baked, and shipped directly from IRGSH. If you can boot it, run it, and hack on it, that’s our revival milestone.
Q: What’s the current progress?
A: Check out the Logs section below for real-time updates and commit-level chronicles.
Q: Mengapa situs web ini tidak ditulis dalam Bahasa Indonesia?
A: Situs web ini akan dibangun ulang dalam multi bahasa setelah kami berhasil membangkitkan proyek. Ngomong-ngomong, kemampuan Berbahasa Inggris adalah peningkatan kapasitas manuasia yang juga menjadi tujuan proyek.
